Private Toolbox

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Private Toolbox ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services.

Private Toolbox is an offline-first productivity application. Most operations are performed entirely on your device without any data transmission.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Processed Locally

The app processes the following data entirely on your device. This data never leaves your device:

  • Documents (PDFs, images) you scan, convert, merge, or modify
  • QR codes and barcodes you scan or generate
  • Images you compress or resize
  • Text you count, transform, hash, or encode
  • Notes you create in the notepad
  • All calculator and converter operations
  • Timer and stopwatch data
Important: None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services. All processing happens locally on your device.

2.2 Data Collected by Third Parties

Advertising Services: The app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Usage data related to ad interactions

For more information about how Google handles this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

2.3 User-Initiated Communications

When you use the "Report Bug" feature, you voluntarily send an email to our support address. This email may contain:

  • Your email address
  • Device information you choose to include
  • Description of the issue

This information is only used to respond to your inquiry and improve the app.

3. How We Use Information

  • Local Processing: All document, image, and text processing is performed locally to provide app functionality
  • Advertising: AdMob uses collected data to display personalized advertisements
  • Bug Reports: Email communications are used solely to address technical issues and improve the app

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All files you process remain on your device and are controlled by you
  • The app does not maintain any servers or databases to store your data
  • We do not have access to the files you process unless you explicitly share them with us via email
  • You can delete any files created by the app at any time through your device's file manager

5. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services: For app ratings and reviews (Privacy Policy)

These services operate independently and are governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan QR codes, barcodes, and documents
  • Storage: To save and load files you create or modify
  • Internet: To display advertisements and open external links

All permissions are used exclusively for their stated purposes and do not result in data collection beyond what is described in this policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Private Toolbox does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed to work entirely offline for core functionality, and we do not actively collect data from users of any age.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control all files and data created by the app on your device
  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Contact us with questions about this privacy policy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
