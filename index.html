Private Toolbox

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Private Toolbox ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services.

Private Toolbox is an offline-first productivity application. Every tool in the app — including the on-device AI chat — processes your data entirely on your own device. The only time the app touches the network is when you explicitly choose to download an AI model file or when you tap an external link.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Processed Locally

The app processes the following data entirely on your device. This data never leaves your device:

  • Documents (PDFs, images) you scan, convert, merge, or modify
  • QR codes and barcodes you scan or generate
  • Images you compress, resize, or convert between formats (PNG, JPG, WebP, etc.)
  • Text you count, transform, hash, or encode
  • Notes you create in the notepad
  • All calculator, graphing calculator, unit converter, and number base operations
  • Timer and stopwatch data
  • Local AI chat conversations: prompts, responses, and chat history are generated and stored entirely on your device. Inference runs locally using the downloaded model — your messages are never transmitted to any server, including ours.
Important: None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services. All processing happens locally on your device.

2.2 On-Device AI Model Downloads

The Local AI Chat feature uses open-source language models that must be downloaded to your device before use. When you explicitly tap "Download" on a model:

  • The app makes a standard HTTPS request to the Hugging Face Hub (huggingface.co) to fetch the model file.
  • As with any HTTPS download, the remote server may log standard request metadata (such as your IP address and user agent) according to its own privacy policy.
  • The app itself does not send any personal information, account data, or your chat content during these downloads — only the model file is requested.
  • Once a model is downloaded, the Local AI Chat runs fully offline. No further network requests are made to use it.

For details on how Hugging Face handles download requests, see Hugging Face's Privacy Policy.

2.3 User-Initiated Communications

When you use the "Report Bug" feature, you voluntarily send an email to our support address. This email may contain:

  • Your email address
  • Device information you choose to include
  • Description of the issue

This information is only used to respond to your inquiry and improve the app.

3. How We Use Information

  • Local Processing: All document, image, text, and AI chat operations are performed locally to provide app functionality.
  • AI Model Downloads: Network requests are made only to retrieve the model files you explicitly choose to download from Hugging Face. No personal data is sent with these requests.
  • Bug Reports: Email communications are used solely to address technical issues and improve the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All files, notes, and AI chat histories you create remain on your device and are controlled by you.
  • The app does not maintain any servers or databases to store your data.
  • We do not have access to the files, messages, or conversations you process unless you explicitly share them with us via email.
  • You can delete any files, notes, or chats created by the app at any time through the app itself or your device's file manager. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data, including any downloaded AI models.

5. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Hugging Face Hub: Used only when you explicitly download an on-device AI model. See Hugging Face Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services: For app installation, updates, and in-app ratings/reviews (Privacy Policy).

These services operate independently and are governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan QR codes, barcodes, and documents.
  • Storage: To save and load files, notes, chat histories, and downloaded AI models.
  • Internet: To download AI model files from Hugging Face when you explicitly request them, and to open external links (such as this privacy policy).

All permissions are used exclusively for their stated purposes and do not result in data collection beyond what is described in this policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Private Toolbox does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed to work entirely offline for core functionality, and we do not actively collect data from users of any age.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control all files, notes, chat histories, and downloaded AI models created or stored by the app on your device.
  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time.
  • Contact us with questions about this privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
